Introduction

Eating fruits provides essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber, which are crucial for digestion and maintaining a healthy gut. This nutrient-rich intake can lower the risk of chronic diseases like heart disease and certain cancers, and help manage blood pressure and weight. Overall, a diet rich in fruits strengthens the immune system and contributes to greater well-being, energy, and even skin health.
